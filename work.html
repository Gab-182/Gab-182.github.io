The Journey of Growth and Learning

I embarked on my professional journey as an agriculture engineer in Beirut, Lebanon, dedicating almost four years to working with a reputable company that provided vital plant pesticide protection to local farmers. This experience allowed me to understand the importance of leveraging technology to enhance efficiency and productivity.

Driven by a passion for programming and software development, I transitioned into the world of technology. As a freelancer, I collaborated with a civil engineering company, where I developed a program using Python ,tkinter and other python libraries. This software simplified the calculation of building base dimensions, empowering engineers with a user-friendly tool.

My journey in technology continued to unfold as I delved into web development. Equipped with a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I took on freelance projects as a front-end web developer, crafting visually appealing and functional websites for clients in Dubai and Abu Dhabi.

The ever-evolving landscape of technology led me to explore the exciting realm of blockchain. Eager to embrace this cutting-edge technology, I joined a boot camp focused on Solana blockchain. This immersive experience equipped me with the skills to create a platform within the Solana ecosystem, facilitating seamless and secure donation campaigns for various entities.

Currently, my passion lies in the realm of machine learning algorithms, AI, and data science. With a thirst for knowledge, I am actively pursuing courses to expand my expertise in these areas. My ultimate aspiration is to channel my skills and passion into a role that allows me to contribute to the field of AI and data science, making a meaningful impact with my work.
As I continue to grow and evolve, I eagerly anticipate the next chapter of my career, where I can apply my diverse skill set and drive innovation in the field of machine learning and AI.
