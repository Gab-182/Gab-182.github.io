The Road I Walked to Shape My Skills

Unleashing Expertise and Driving into Innovation

Agricultural Engineering Foundation: My four-year tenure as an agriculture engineer in Beirut, Lebanon provided me with a solid foundation in problem-solving, analytical thinking, and a deep understanding of the agricultural industry. This experience instilled in me the value of precision and efficiency.

Transitioning to Technology: Fueling my passion for programming and software development, I embarked on a new path. As a freelancer, I collaborated with a civil engineering company, where I developed a program using Python and tkinter. This venture enabled me to combine my technical skills with my industry knowledge, resulting in the creation of a user-friendly tool that streamlined building base dimension calculations for engineers.

Front-End Web Development: Eager to explore the vast possibilities of the web, I delved into the world of front-end web development. Armed with expertise in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I embarked on freelance projects, crafting visually appealing and functionally robust websites for clients in Dubai and Abu Dhabi. This venture expanded my horizons and deepened my understanding of decentralized technologies.

Through dedicated courses and hands-on projects, I am cultivating a comprehensive skill set to contribute to the cutting-edge advancements in these fields. I am excited about the potential of leveraging AI and data science to make a significant impact in diverse domains.
